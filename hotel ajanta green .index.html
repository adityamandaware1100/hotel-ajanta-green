Our Story

A Heritage Hideaway Near Ajanta Caves

Nestled near the iconic Ajanta T-Point in Fardapur, Hotel Ajanta Green Restaurant and Resort is your perfect retreat after exploring the ancient wonders of the Ajanta Caves. We blend traditional Indian hospitality with modern comforts to create an unforgettable stay.

Whether you're a pilgrim, history enthusiast, or leisure traveler, our resort offers a serene ambiance, delicious cuisine, and warm service — just minutes from the UNESCO World Heritage Site.
