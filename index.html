Our Story

A Heritage Hideaway Near Ajanta Caves

Nestled near Ajanta T-Point in Fardapur, Hotel Ajanta Green Restaurant and Resort offers 15 well-appointed Deluxe AC Rooms — your perfect retreat after exploring the ancient Ajanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site just 500 meters away.

We blend traditional Indian hospitality with modern comforts. Whether you're a pilgrim, history enthusiast, or leisure traveler, enjoy our serene ambiance, delicious cuisine, and warm service.
