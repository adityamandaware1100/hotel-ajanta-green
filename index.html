🗺️ Visitor Guide

How to Reach Us

Simple directions from Sambhaji Nagar Airport to our hotel & Ajanta Caves

1

🛫 Arrive at Airport

Land at Sambhaji Nagar Airport (Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar).
Taxi & Bus both available outside the terminal.

→ 109 km to Hotel

⏱️ Approx 2 hrs 20 min

2

🏨 Reach Hotel Ajanta Green

Take a taxi or bus from Airport to Ajanta T-Point, Fardapur. Our hotel is right near the T-Point.

Hotel at Ajanta T-Point

📍 Fardapur, Maharashtra 431118

3

🎫 Visit Ajanta Caves

From hotel, walk 500m to Ajanta T-Point. Buy a bus ticket there and travel 4 km inside to the caves.

🎟️ Bus/Entry Ticket: ₹120 approx

🚶 500m walk → 🚌 4km bus ride
